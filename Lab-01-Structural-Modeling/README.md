# Lab 01 – Structural Modeling

## Experiments

1. Half Adder
2. Full Adder
3. 4-bit Ripple Carry Adder

## Tools Used

- Verilog HDL
- Xilinx Vivado
- Basys-3 FPGA

## Folder Structure

- rtl/ – Verilog source files
- tb/ – Testbenches
- simulation/ – Behavioral and post-implementation simulation results
- schematic/ – RTL and synthesized schematics
- timing/ – Timing reports and delay results

## Objectives

- Understand structural modeling in Verilog.
- Design and verify basic combinational circuits.
- Analyze post-synthesis timing and implementation results.
